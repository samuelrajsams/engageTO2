How do Firefox Web Desktop Notifications Work?

Insert your Smart Code

You’ll get a Smart Code immediately after creating your free EngageTo account. Place this Smart Code before the closing </head> tag on your web page.

Enable Opt-in

An opt-in box appears when a visitor opens your website. It asks for permission to send Push Notifications to the visitors' Firefox browser. The moment the visitor clicks “Allow”, they get added to your subscriber list. No email ID or other personal details required. Simple and efficient.

Set up to send your first notification

You’re now ready to send your first Push Notification to your users. Open your EngageTo dashboard, write the title and the message you want your users to receive from your website. Hit Send! Sit back and wait for the push notification to work its magic.

Ta-da!

Your subscribers can now view your Push Notifications. You can keep a tab on your notifications through your EngageTo dashboard. The dashboard also lets you view the number of clicks on your Push Notifications and other analyzed data. Yup, you’ve found your unicorn.
