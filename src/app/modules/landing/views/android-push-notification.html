Steps to Operate Android Notifications

Place your Smart Code

As soon as you create your Free EngageTo account, a Smart Code gets generated. Put this Smart Code before the closing </head> tag on your web page.

Allow Opt-in

When your website has a visitor, an opt-in box pops up for them. This opt-in box asks for permission to send Push Notifications to your website visitor. When the visitor clicks “Allow”, they’ll become your mobile website subscriber. Get subscribers without asking for Email ID and other details.

Send your first notification

You’re set to send your first mobile Push Notification to users. Access your EngageTo dashboard, compose the title and the message of your Push Notification, and click 'Send'. There goes your first Push Notification.

Victory!

Your subscribers can now view your push message on their Android devices. Keep track of your push notifications through your EngageTo dashboard. See the number of clicks on your push notifications and other metrics, optimise and do even better!
