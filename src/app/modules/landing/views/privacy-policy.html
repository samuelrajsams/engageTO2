Privacy Policy

Like most website operators, engageto collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request, Country, City etc. Engageto’s purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how engageto’s visitors use its website & also to provide our customer better service.

Engageto also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on our blogs. Engageto only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that blog commenter IP addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the blog where the comment was left.

Engageto does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. 5. COOKIES

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Engageto uses cookies to help engageto identify and track visitors & also push subscribers, their usage of engageto website, and their website access preferences. Engageto website visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using engageto’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of engageto’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
