How to Enable Chrome Notifications for Desktop and Mobile

Insert your Smart Code

The moment you create your free EngageTo account, you’ll receive a Smart Code. Place it before the closing </head> tag on your website page.

Opt-in activation

When a visitor lands on your website, they receive an opt-in box asking for permission to send Push Notifications on their Chrome browser. When the visitor clicks on “Allow”, he/she will be added to your subscriber list. You skip the tedious task of asking for email address or other details.

Send your first notification

You’re now set to send your very first EngageTo Push Notification. Just log in to your EngageTo dashboard, compose a title and message for the Push Notification and you're ready to send!

And that's done!

Once you click on send, your visitors will see your notification in real time. You can track your Push Notifications on your EngageTo dashboard. You can also track the number of clicks on your notifications and other metrics.
