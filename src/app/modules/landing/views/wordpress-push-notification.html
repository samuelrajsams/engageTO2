See How EngageTo WordPress Plugin Works

Create your Free EngageTo Account and get your Account ID

Create your Free EngageTo Account. Then, on your Dashboard, click on the “Account Details” on the left of the screen and you’ll see your Account ID.

Add the EngageTo plugin to your WordPress dashboard

Log in to your WordPress dashboard and go to Plugins. Click on “Add New” on the left side. Now search for EngageTo in the search bar, select the plugin and click on “Install Now”. You’ve now installed EngageTo on your WordPress website. (If you want to download the plugin files directly, go to the WordPress plugin page.)

Trigger plugin and add your Account ID

Once you’ve installed the EngageTo plugin, you will be prompted to start the plugin. After doing so, go to Settings, click on EngageTo and type your Account ID (Refer to Step 1). You’re ready to use EngageTo.

Increase in click rate

The moment there’s a visitor on your website, an opt-in box will appear. If the visitor clicks on “Allow”, they will automatically get added to your subscriber list. You can personalize the opt-in message and the time after which the opt-in box appears.

Send your First Notification

You’re set to send your first notification! Just log in to your EngageTo dashboard, compose a title and a crisp message for the Push Notification and hit send! It's that easy to send Push Notifications.

Track the results

Your visitors will see the Push Notification in real-time. You can track the number of notifications delivered, the click-rate, and many other metrics on your EngageTo dashboard.
