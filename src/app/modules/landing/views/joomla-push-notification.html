Check Out How Push Notifications For Joomla Work

Get your Account ID by creating Your Free EngageTo account

Create your Free EngageTo Account and go to your dashboard. On the left side of the panel, click on “Account Details”. This will display your Account ID.

Add the EngageTo plugin to your Joomla dashboard

Log in to your Joomla admin dashboard, and click on “Manage”, under “Extensions”. Now, search for EngageTo in the "Install from Web" search bar, select the plugin and click on Install.

Initiate plugin and put your Account ID

Activate the plugin post installing the EngageTo plugin. For this, head to Extensions, then Plugins and search for 'EngageTo'. Select the plugin and change status to 'Enabled'. Now type your Account ID (see Step 1). Lastly, click Save & then Close.

Get high on increasing click-rate!

The moment a visitor lands on your website, an opt-in box will pop, asking the visitor to subscribe to Push Notifications from your website. If the visitor clicks on “Allow”, they are added to your subscriber list. You’ll see the jump in subscriber count on the EngageTo dashboard.

You’ve done it! *Drum-roll*

Congratulations! You're set to send your first Push Notification. Your subscribers will see the push notification in real time. Track push notification deliveries, click rate and other metrics on your dashboard.
