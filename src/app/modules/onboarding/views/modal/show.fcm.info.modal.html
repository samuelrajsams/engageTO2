Steps to add FCM credentials:

1. If you've gmail account, go to https://console.firebase.google.com/ and sign in using your gmail credentials. (You can also Google Apps for Work(Gsuite) for this). If you don't have gmail account, create new free gmail account by going to https://accounts.google.com/Signup?service=mail

2. After creating account visit https://console.firebase.google.com

3. Click on 'CREATE NEW PROJECT' and Enter your project (website) name and select your country/region. Click on 'CREATE PROJECT' button.

4. You'll find gear icon in front of your project name at left sidebar. Click on gear icon and go to 'Project Settings'.

5. From General tab copy 'Web API Key' and paste it in first text box.

6. Click 'CLOUD MESSAGING' tab, copy 'Server Key' and paste them in below text boxes respectively.
