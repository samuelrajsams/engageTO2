Why should you add your FCM Project Number and FCM API Key?

To send push notifications, Chrome requires signing up for a free FCM (Firebase Cloud Messaging) account. Currently, the entire PushCrew user-base is using our account. This means that your subscriber list is attached to the PushCrew FCM account. We therefore recommend that you sign up for your own FCM account and subscribe your users to it. This will ensure that you are able to export your subscriber list conveniently as and when you want. Without it, you wouldn't be able to export your subscriber list.

As of now, there's no limit on the number of subscribers or the number of push notifications in the FCM projects. Going forward, if Chrome adds a limit to these numbers, your account might need to share the limit with all of our other users. Hence, it's recommended that you add your own FCM Project Number and FCM API Key to your PushCrew account.
